Calculator for elementary functions (and other stuff)

Syntax: function(a + bi, c + di)
Supported functions:
  • Trigonometric: sin(z); cos(z); tan(z)
  • Inverse Trigonometric: arcsin(z); arccos(z); arctan(z)
  • Hyperbolic Trigonometric: sinh(z); cosh(z); tanh(z)
  • Exponential: exp(z)
  • Logarithmic: log(a, b); ln(x)

  • Factorial: fac(n)
  • R choose K: rCk(r, k)
  • Complex Addition: cadd(z1, z2)
  • Complex Negation: cneg(z1, z2)
  • Complex Multiplication: cmul(z1, z2)
  • Complex Division: cdiv(z1, z2)
  • Complex Exponentiation: cpow(z, n)
